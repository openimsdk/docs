# 💬 Our Mission

**Break free from cloud lock-in. Define your own communication. Empower every team to build and control their own instant messaging system.**

In an era dominated by cloud service giants, more and more organizations find themselves locked into closed communication platforms:
rising costs, no control over data, and limited room for innovation.

**OpenIMSDK's mission is to break these barriers.**

With a fully open-source instant messaging infrastructure, developers can:
- 🧩 Deploy communication systems on their own servers
- ⚙️ Freely customize messaging logic and interaction patterns
- 💡 Rapidly build custom IM products through secondary development

---

# 🤖 The AIGC Era

With the rise of AIGC, **the chat window is becoming the primary interface for human-agent interaction.**
Whether it's AI assistants, intelligent customer service, virtual colleagues, or voice agents,
the conversational interface has become the most natural way for users to interact with AI services.

As a result, **instant messaging infrastructure is no longer just a "message channel" — it is the core medium for AI interaction.**

As an open and controllable communication engine,
OpenIMSDK provides a solid technical foundation for this trend, enabling developers to:
- 🧠 Enable AI and users to collaborate efficiently within a unified chat interface
- 🗣️ Seamlessly integrate voice, text, and multimodal interactions
- 🧩 Easily integrate multi-agent systems into business workflows
- 🔐 Run AI-powered chat and knowledge assistants in private environments, ensuring data security and compliance

> **OpenIMSDK = IM Infrastructure + AI Interaction Gateway**

# 💡 What We Believe

- Instant messaging should not be "rented" from cloud services — it should be **self-owned**
- Developers should not be constrained by closed APIs — they should have **full control over data and logic**
- Every team deserves the right to **define their own communication experience**

# ⚙️ Our Goals

- Build instant messaging infrastructure that is **self-hostable and deeply customizable**
- Provide an **IMSDK + IMServer integrated open-source framework** that lowers the barrier to building your own IM
- Support **full-spectrum scalability from lightweight chat to enterprise-grade systems**
- Enable developers to freely assemble, modify, and control their communication systems like building blocks
